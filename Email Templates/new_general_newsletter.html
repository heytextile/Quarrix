Quarrix Building Products is a member of the Liberty Diversified International (LDI) family of companies and a brand within LDI’s Liberty Plastics, Inc. business unit. Founded in 1985 with LDI's support and driven by a shared passion for innovation, Quarrix has developed several patented designs that have produced numerous advanced building materials and breakthroughs within the roofing industry.
